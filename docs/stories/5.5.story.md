# Story 5.5: GoHighLevel CRM Integration for Leads

## Story

**As a** operator (Fernando),
**I want** all FunWheel leads synced with GoHighLevel CRM,
**so that** I can manage follow-ups, automations, and community access in one place.

## Status

Approved

## Context

**Epic 5 — Sistema 2: FunWheel (A-R-T)**

Dependência de 5.3 e 5.4 (leads capturados). Consolida a integração GoHighLevel — implementada incrementalmente nas stories 5.3 e 5.4 — em um cliente robusto e testado.

- [ ] **AC-1:** `packages/integrations/src/gohighlevel/ghl-client.ts` completo com:
